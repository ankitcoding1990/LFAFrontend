About Edward

Edward Robinson is a founder and co-managing member of Robinson Kardon. He focuses his practice on criminal defense, in both state and federal courts, on behalf of clients who stand accused of white collar, corporate and general criminal conduct. He is a certified specialist in Criminal Law by the State Bar of California.

While Edward has worked extensively at both the state and federal level since starting his criminal defense practice in 1988, he has a rare and impressive portfolio of federal cases, having represented clients in hundreds of complex cases across the country, including California, Hawaii, New York, Oregon, Texas, Arizona, Washington, Nebraska, Massachusetts, Florida, Arkansas, Wisconsin, Colorado, Georgia and Nevada. He has also appeared countless times in California State Courts on matters related to both serious felony charges and all types of misdemeanors.
